Origami Doubts

Giraffe

Instuctions

Step 1) Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

Step 2) Fold the sides of the paper in to the centre.

Step 3) Fold the paper in half.

Step 4) Rotate the paper about 45 degrees.

Step 5) Make an Outside Reverse Fold along the dotted line.

Step 6) Make another Outside Reverse Fold along the dotted line.

Step 7) Make an Inside Reverse Fold on the tip of the head.

Step 8) Make a Crimp Fold on the back to form the tail.

Step 9) Make a small cut through both layers of paper along the dotted line to form the legs.
